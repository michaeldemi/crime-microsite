Part 2

Low-Cost DIY Security Tips

1. Reinforce Your Strike Plate

Replace the small screws in your door's strike plate with 3-inch screws that anchor into the door frame stud. This simple fix makes your door vastly more resistant to being kicked in.

2. Install a Patio Door Security Bar

This is a must-have. A simple, pressure-mounted bar makes it nearly impossible to force a sliding door open from the outside.

3. Use Motion-Sensor Lighting

Install inexpensive solar or wired motion-sensor lights above your garage and back patio. Darkness is a burglar's best friend.
